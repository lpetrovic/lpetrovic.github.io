About me

I am a PhD student in robotics focused on developing novel trajectory optimization methods for high dimensional robot motion planning. I am a member of the Laboratory for Autonomous Systems and Mobile Robotics (LAMOR) and a teaching assistant at the University of Zagreb. I am particularly interested in algorithms that generate smooth and natural motion for high degree-of-freedom robots that operate in complex and dynamic environments, with the goal of achieving safe autonomy.

Teaching

I am a teaching assistant at the University of Zagreb. I co-supervised one graduate and five undergraduate students and assist in the following courses:

Robot programming and simulation (at Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computing)

  • This course gives a practical approach to basics of robot and environment modelling and simulation, while simultaneously teaching students how to interface and control robotic systems. The course introduces students to the basics of the Robot operating system (ROS) and programming ROS nodes in C++ and Python.

Machine learning (at Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computing)

  • This course provides an in-depth coverage of the theory and principles of machine learning. The course covers two major approaches to machine learning: supervised learning (classification and regression) and unsupervised learning (clustering and dimensionality reduction). We give a systematic insight into the key models from the generative, discriminative parametric, and nonparametric flavors.

Introduction to systems and automatic control (at the Croatian Military Academy).

  • The aim of the course is to introduce students to system and control theory in the context of military and defence applications. We introduce the basic elements of automatic control systems (sensors, regulators and actuators) and the fundamental properties of stability, controllability and observability.

My responsibilities include helping out in curriculum development, conducting laboratory exercises and grading exams. Previously, I was a teaching assistant for the following courses

  • Computer-controlled systems
  • Laboratory and skills - Matlab
  • Computer architecture 1
