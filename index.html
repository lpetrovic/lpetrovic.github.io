About me

I am a PhD student in robotics, focused on trajectory optimization methods for high dimensional motion planning. I am a member of the Laboratory for Autonomous Systems and Mobile Robotics (LAMOR) and a teaching assistant at University of Zagreb.

Interests

  • robotics
  • motion planning
  • mobile robotics
  • trajectory optimization
